Grilled cheese

A simple childhood comfort food

Ingredients

  • 2 slices favorite bread. I prefer a thick Texas toast.
  • 2 slices favorite cheese. A combination can work wonderfully. Gouda and cheddar are my go-to.
  • 1 tbs butter

Steps

  1. Heat a frying pan over medium heat. Butter both slices of bread and assemble sandwich with cheese between bread slices and buttered sides on the outside.
  2. Place sandwich into warmed pan. Let sit until golden brown, flip and repeat.
  3. Remove from heat, cut at a diagonal and enjoy! Serve with tomato soup for best experience.
